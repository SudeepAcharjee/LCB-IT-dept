About

IT department || LCBC

The self - financed courses was initially started under the roof of Department of Computer Science. The professional courses like Bachelors in Computer Application (BCA) and Bachelors of Information technology (BSc-IT) was started in the year 2006 and 2008 respectively. A new genre of manpower / managers is required to harness the awesome capabilities of Information Technology and to tap the potential of this tool to its maximum. The BCA and Bsc-IT courses is designed and introduced by Gauhati University to bridge this gap and produce employable graduate in Information technology which will enable the industry to grow and he graduates to become successful in the field of Information Technology.
